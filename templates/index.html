Features

Expert Care at Your Fingertips

At eTele Mo, we are committed to provide accessible, reliable, and patient-centered telehealth services tailored to meet the support and care every patient needs. Our services include:

Symptom Assessment and Initial Health Evaluation

Our eTele Mo team conducts a preliminary assessment based on patient's reported symptoms and concern to provide guidance on possible conditions and recommend appropriate course of action for quality care.

Online Medical Consultations

eTele Mo facilitates virtual consultations with our licensed healthcare providers regarding patient common and non-emergency health concerns. We provide a safe and secure online environment where patients can discuss their health conditions freely.

Medical Advice and Treatment Guidance

Based on the consultation and symptom assessment, we offer appropriate medical advice and recommendations for home management and treatment of minor health conditions.

Prescription and Medical Guidance

When clinically appropriate, we can issue medical prescriptions or provide guidance to help you understand the importance and proper use of your prescribed medications issued by your healthcare provider.

Health Education and Preventive Counseling

Our eTele Mo team provides reliable and evidence-based health advice to help you understand your health conditions better. We also give educational information to guide you in your journey to wellness and encourage healthy practices.

About Us

Serving Healthcare Professionals

In today's fast-paced world, clicking, scrolling, and tapping has become second nature. From online shopping to virtual meetings, convenience is everywhere. Yet when it comes to healthcare, many still face long waiting times, unnecessary clinic visits, and complicated scheduling. Isn't it time healthcare caught up with the digital world?

Introducing eTele Mo — tell mo, sakit mo — a telehealth platform that brings healthcare directly to you. With just a few clicks, you can consult licensed healthcare professionals, receive guidance, and manage non-urgent health concerns without leaving your home. Think of it as your personal health assistant, available anytime, anywhere!
